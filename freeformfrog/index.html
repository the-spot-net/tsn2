A Bit About the Freeform Frog

Ok, so Freeform Frog is somewhat of an abstract title. That's ok. That is because this store is somewhat of an abstract store. It is hosted online by a third party (cafepress.com), and the items up for sale have yet to be made. We at tsn make the logos and images, based on your feedback in the forums. We then upload the graphics to a specific item, and when you purchase your item, they make it and send it to you.

The name was suggested to me as an available nickname when I tried to enter a chat room. It coincided with the new logo for this site, and I liked the concept. So now it is the name of our online store.

This is just the beginning though. Once we find out how well this store goes over, we will expand the items that are available for purchase, and hopefully, by next year, will be able to design and host our own online store. For those of you that know me personally, you know that this type of thing is what I have been working on doing ever since our first endeavourer with a college-student-run website. So, naturally, this site has big plans, and there is now nothing to hinder its development, but maybe finances. (But we will worry about that later.)

The store will be open for business on a date, to-be-announced, during the summer or fall. As of right now, we are not planning on making any profit off the items for sale so you will have the lowest price available. Perhaps when things work out better, we will be able to get a couple more stores and offer more items. Until then, enjoy.

- Neo
