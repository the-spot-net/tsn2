Monday - May 26, 2003

This morning, I woke up late, at about 8:45, and was supposed to call Stacy at 8:30. She had been awake since 7:00 this morning, and just didn't get out of bed until 8:30 or so. So, I went to her house to get her about 9:15, and brought her back to my house.

When we got there, I didn't want to clean my room...I wanted to sit with her and spend time with her. So we did, and we played around some, until my dad came up to check on us, and was a little disappointed that we didn't get anything done in my room, as far as moving my things in. So I put together a shelf and put my software on it, as well as my music.

We worked for about 30 minutes, or so, and then we went to the park for a spontaneous picnic. We had some of the cold cuts from the weekend, and some sodas. It was a nice day out, except for the fact that it got humid when it didn't rain, and just sprinkled. So we only stayed at the park for a little bit - primarily because I told my parents a time that I would be back, and because I had to go to work, with enough time to drop her off at her house on the way.

At work tonight, I was Amber's runner...and I will never again fill that position. She was doing well with getting the orders accurate on the screen, but I noticed that she was having trouble with putting some of the specifics on there so that I could make it like the people wanted. So, I told her "I hope you are putting them on there right, so I don't have to make them again..." And she said, "I hope you are reading them right." And from that point on, she left out several specifics for the orders that caused me a lot of problems, especially since the vanilla ice cream, which is in just about everything, was rock hard. It as terrible tonight...but now that I am home, I feel better, because I can rest.

I called Stacy tonight, and her phone died after a 20 minute conversation. It's ok though, because she needs to sleep, as well as I do. I also looked at my computer, and it stopped at 35% for the formatting, and said "Disk not ready. Formatting Terminated." Stupid homosexual computer. I told it it had better get past 35% by the time I got home tonight (it was at 32% at that time)...and it stops right on 35%. Crazy thing. I am restarting the format, to see what happens, and hopefully it will work, or go through the stuff it has already done a lot quicker.

That's all for tonight. I have to go to work tomorrow morning, so I am going to bed early tonight. Laterz.
